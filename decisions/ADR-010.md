Database reactivation campaigns use a two-stage permission-pass model to comply with AU Spam Act 2003 and Privacy Act 1988. Stage 1: send a single permission-pass SMS asking contacts to opt in. Stage 2: only contacts who explicitly opt in receive the full reactivation campaign. All numbers must be washed against the DNCR (Do Not Call Register) before any voice outreach. Business numbers are exempt from DNCR but personal mobiles used for business are not. SMS Sender ID Register compliance required from 1 July 2026 for alphanumeric sender IDs.
