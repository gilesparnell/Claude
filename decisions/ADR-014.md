New customers get a 90-day pro-rata money-back guarantee on their subscription. If they cancel within 90 days, they receive a refund for the unused portion of their billing period. The $2,000 setup fee is non-refundable — it covers real delivery work (GHL build, voice agent config, onboarding). This reduces buyer friction significantly for a high-ticket SaaS without exposing setup costs to refund risk. Worst case on Business Annual: customer cancels at day 89, refund ~$4,510, keep ~$1,480 + $1,000 setup = $2,480 collected for 3 months of service.
