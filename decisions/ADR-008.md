Structured data uses Organisation + SoftwareApplication + FAQPage schema, not LocalBusiness. Parnell Systems is a remote SaaS business with no physical office — LocalBusiness would be factually incorrect and could trigger Google penalties. SoftwareApplication schema supports pricing, ratings, and feature data. FAQPage schema is highest-impact for SERP real estate on the pricing page.
