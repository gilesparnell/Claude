The `/insights` report on 2026-04-21 recommended a verified-ship workflow and an explicit session-resumption procedure. Both could have been shell commands under `~/.claude/commands/`, but were shipped as skills at `~/.claude/skills/ship/` and `~/.claude/skills/resume/` instead.

Skills compose with the existing `skill-forced-eval-hook.sh` on `UserPromptSubmit`, show up in the live skill list, and integrate with other skills via declared triggers. Commands are simpler but don't plug into the skill-eval flow. `/ship` also composes with the existing `verification-before-completion` skill for its final verification step rather than re-implementing `gh pr view` — composition over duplication means `/ship` improves automatically when `verification-before-completion` improves. Portal stubs live at `claude-artefacts/skills/ship/` and `claude-artefacts/skills/resume/` and are rendered to the portal by the GitHub Action.
