Three flat-rate tiers: Starter ($399/mo), Business ($599/mo), Professional ($799/mo). Annual billing at 1 month free (~8% discount) using a single Stripe annual Price object per tier (11× monthly), not coupons or subscription schedules.

Setup fee: $2,000 for all tiers (covers GHL sub-account, voice agent build, phone number, workflows, testing, onboarding call). Annual setup discounts push customers toward higher tiers and longer commitment:

- Starter Annual: full $2,000 setup fee applies
- Business Annual: 50% off setup ($1,000)
- Professional Annual: setup fee fully waived

Year 1 totals (subscription + setup):
- Starter Monthly: $6,788 | Starter Annual: $6,389 (saves $399)
- Business Monthly: $9,188 | Business Annual: $7,589 (saves $1,599)
- Professional Monthly: $11,588 | Professional Annual: $8,789 (saves $2,799)

Monthly billing always pays full $2,000 setup. Existing customers at prior pricing get a 90-day loyalty bridge before transitioning to new tiers.
