Three flat-rate tiers: Starter ($399/mo), Business ($599/mo), Professional ($799/mo). Annual billing at 2 months free (~17% discount) using a single Stripe annual Price object per tier (10x monthly), not coupons or subscription schedules. Setup fee of $499 shown on monthly view, strikethrough on annual toggle as an incentive. Existing customers at prior pricing get a 90-day loyalty bridge before transitioning to new tiers.
