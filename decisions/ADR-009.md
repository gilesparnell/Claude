Three flat-rate tiers: Starter ($399/mo), Business ($599/mo), Professional ($799/mo). Annual billing at 2 months free (~17% discount) using a single Stripe annual Price object per tier (10× monthly), not coupons or subscription schedules.

Setup fee: $2,000 for all tiers (covers GHL sub-account, voice agent build, phone number, workflows, testing, onboarding call). Annual setup discounts push customers toward higher tiers and longer commitment:

- Starter Annual: full $2,000 setup fee applies
- Business Annual: 50% off setup ($1,000)
- Professional Annual: setup fee fully waived

Year 1 totals: Starter Annual $5,990, Business Annual $6,990, Professional Annual $7,990. Monthly billing always pays full $2,000 setup.

Existing customers at prior pricing get a 90-day loyalty bridge before transitioning to new tiers.
