All customer voice agents are delivered via Go High Level's native voice agent functionality. LiveKit and Vapi are future-state options for custom deployments requiring more control, but GHL native is the current platform for all sales, demos, and client deployments. Plans, demos, and marketing materials must reflect this — do not reference LiveKit/Vapi as current capabilities.
