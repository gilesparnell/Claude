Comp floor for the active 2026 job search: **$16k/month post-tax** (= "current lifestyle", not survival).

Backs out to:
- **$298,374 AU gross cash salary** (= net $192,000/year after AU FY 2025-26 resident tax + Medicare Levy 2%)
- **OR ~$334k TRP / package** if quoted package-inclusive (cash + 12% super on top)
- Assumes: private health insurance held (no MLS), no HECS/HELP debt, no salary sacrifice into super

**Why:** Giles is under genuine financial pressure but the $16k is lifestyle-current, not survival-minimum. He explicitly didn't quote the survival number. Recommendations should not assume he'd flex below this without him telling you.

**How to apply:**
- Any role discussion: if listed comp is below $298k cash → flag the gap explicitly, don't paper over it
- Excludes him from mid-Senior PM / BA / PO seats at AU startups (those cap ~$220k base)
- Right band is **Head of Product / VP Product / Director / Operating Partner / Field CTO / Strategic TAM** — these clear the floor; the lower-seniority "passion play" roles don't
- A "$X + super" job ad means $X cash + 12% super on top, NOT total package. A "$X package" or "$X TRP" means super included
- Worked example: a "$250k + super" listing = $250k cash = $13,864/month net — **$2,136/month short** of his floor
