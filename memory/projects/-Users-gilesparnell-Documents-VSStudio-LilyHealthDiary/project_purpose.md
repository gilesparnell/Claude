The app is being built so the user's daughter (Lily) can track her food intake to determine if anything in her diet is causing her abdominal pains. The primary clinical value is correlating specific foods with symptom onset, severity, and timing. A health practitioner will review exported diary data to identify patterns. Photos are secondary to structured symptom/food data.
