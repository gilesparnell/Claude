**Fact:** Giles confirmed on 2026-04-14 that the voice AI product (formerly AWE2M8, brand TBD) is being pitched and positioned for the **US market**. Australian customers are proof-of-concept and unit economics validation — they are not the target market for the next phase of growth.

**Why:** The US SMB market is ~30 million businesses vs Australia's ~2.5 million. The friend Giles is talking to is US-based and is lining up US investors. The 2026-04-15 call is the first of those conversations. Capital, customers, and reseller channel are all US-focused for the next 12+ months.

**How to apply:**

1. **Don't lean on the AU compliance moat (ACMA bundles, Telnyx Sydney PoP, Privacy Act) when discussing differentiation.** That moat exists for the AU market only and does not transfer to US positioning. Earlier synthesis I wrote (~/Documents/investor-call-prep-2026-04-15.md) leaned heavily on this and is now incorrect for the US framing.

2. **The replacement differentiators** for US positioning are:
   - **Already paying customers in a smaller market with zero CAC** — proves the model works before US-scale capital
   - **Founder shape** — Giles is a 50-year-old operator with 13 years at AWS, not a 25-year-old wrapper-founder. AWS heritage is American credibility currency.
   - **Platform-by-architecture** — self-serve + Stripe Connect + reseller white-label is load-bearing in the roadmap, not a service business with a wizard
   - **Capital efficiency via agentic dev** — $750k goes 3-4× further with AI-assisted build than a typical voice AI raise
   - **Vertical depth** — gym, tradie, real estate prompt packs vs generic horizontal voice AI

3. **The honest weakness for US framing:** the voice AI category in the US is genuinely crowded (Vapi, Retell, Bland, Synthflow, PolyAI, plus YC batch wrappers every quarter). The "crowded category, empty niche" framing must be earned by picking specific verticals where competitors are shallow, NOT by pretending the broader category isn't crowded.

4. **AU customers translate as:** "We started in Australia because we knew the market and wanted real risk on the line. Two paying customers, growing, retaining, no marketing spend. Now we're bringing the proven model to a market that's 12× the size."

5. **US verticals to check competitor depth on:** Housecall Pro (trades), Jobber (trades), Service Titan (trades), kvCORE (real estate), Follow Up Boss (real estate), Mindbody (fitness), ClubReady (fitness). These are the integration surfaces that matter for US SMB voice AI — not the AU equivalents.
